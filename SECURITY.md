## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security vulnerabilities seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible.

**How to Report:**

1.  **Do not disclose publicly:** Please refrain from disclosing the vulnerability publicly until we have had a chance to address it.
2.  **Email us:** Send an email to `security@then.run` with a detailed description of the vulnerability, including:
    *   Steps to reproduce the issue.
    *   Impact of the vulnerability.
    *   Any potential mitigation strategies.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to addressing reported vulnerabilities in a timely manner. We will keep you informed of our progress and will notify you once the issue has been resolved.
